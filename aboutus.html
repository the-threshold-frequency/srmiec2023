ABOUT US

SRM Indian Energy Conference is a platform that works on the principles and guidlines of a Model United Nations. The 7th edition of SRMIEC will be conducted on the 8th and 9th of October 2021 to involve, evolve and aggregate the ideas of the youth. A conference that exists to assist you in voicing your opinions to the world.
