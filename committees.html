Committees

CCC

The Continuous Crisis Committeeis a committee whose decisions and actions immediately affect the perpetual crisis at hand, thus providing for an experience like no other. The ritual raising of placards is replaced with international espionage, fast-paced decision-making, and incessant debates; all aimed to destroy the ideological enemy.

IAEC

The IAEC stands for International Atomic Energy Council. IAEC strives to discuss and deliberate the role of Atomic energy in Global peace, international security and energy independence.

Know More

INEC

The INEC or the Indian National energy council is a council set up by the Indian government to help define and understand energy crisis in India and formulate sustainable energy plans. They're also tasked to deliberate and recommend developments and analysis of the current energy scenario in the Indian subcontinent.

Know More

CESC

The Council for Environmental Sustainability and Conservation prioritizes a sustainable future. With the resources we currently have and the constantly rising threat of an environmental crisis, this council emphasizes and aims to achieve the sustainability goals and conserve the environment with utmost urgency.
