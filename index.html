SECRETARY GENERAL

As an aspiring engineer, I was given the opportunity to be affiliated with SRM Indian Energy Conference (SRM IEC) since the very first year of my college. I assumed it would give me insights into public speaking and help me develop awareness about the tackling of energy-related issues by countries across the globe. Needless to say, while being a part of this conference, I did more than just improve my debating skills and recognized the existence of ingenious solutions to the problems we are facing. Being someone who has been acquainted with Model United Nations (MUN) before, I realized that SRM IEC works on the same principles as that of a MUN. Yet, what sets it apart is the platform it providesâ€” to discuss and deliberate on energy and its future across the seven seas. I work with hope and dedication that this realization of problems will help us take a step ahead in innovating and creating solutions that will generate a successful and positive impact in our world.

SRM IEC is a conference that is one of a kind; it aims to save, renew, and conquer energy. Over the past 6 editions, we have hosted remarkable competition across different types of committees that have helped us set a benchmark and go beyond the traditional boundaries of a MUN. We, at SRM IEC, aim to provide you with a chance to represent countries, organizations, or individuals at a conference simulating international and national level discussions and, in turn, work on crucial predicaments and solutions for energy and its relevant sectors. That being said, I cordially invite you to come and conquer energy with the 7th edition of SRM IEC 2022 on 8th and 9th October 2022.

Vaibhav Sharma
SECRETARY GENERAL
